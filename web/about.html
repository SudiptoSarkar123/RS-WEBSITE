About Us

Welcome to Taste of Bengal - a culinary journey to the heart of Bengali cuisine!

At Taste of Bengal, we take immense pride in bringing you the authentic flavors of West Bengal, right to your plate. Our restaurant is a celebration of the rich cultural heritage and exquisite culinary traditions that define Bengali food.
Indulge your senses in a symphony of spices and aromas that pay homage to the diverse range of dishes that Bengal has to offer. From the iconic Fish Curry and Mustard Sauce to the delectable Puchka (pani puri) infused with tangy tamarind water, every bite tells a story of generations of culinary perfection.
Our team of experienced chefs, hailing from the heart of Bengal, meticulously craft each dish with a blend of traditional recipes and a modern touch. Whether it's the mouthwatering Hilsa fish or the irresistible sweets like Rasgulla and Sandesh, every dish is a masterpiece that reflects the artistry of Bengali cooking.
Step into our warm and inviting ambience, adorned with artistic elements that evoke the essence of Bengal's rustic charm. Whether you're here for a family gathering, a cozy dinner for two, or simply to satisfy your cravings for authentic Bengali comfort food, Taste of Bengal is your ultimate destination.
We source only the freshest ingredients, ensuring that the flavors burst forth with every bite. Our commitment to quality and taste has made us a beloved establishment among Bengali food enthusiasts and newcomers alike.
Join us at Taste of Bengal, where every dish carries a piece of Bengal's heart and soul. Allow us to take you on a culinary expedition that will leave an indelible mark on your taste buds. Come, experience the true Taste of Bengal like never before!
